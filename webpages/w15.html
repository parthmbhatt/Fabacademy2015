what will it do?

My project hasnt changed much since the start, (except that instead of a mechanical firing system I'll be using an electronagnetic one). I'll be attempting to build an automatic 2DOF coilgun turret. what this Implies is:

  • it will be able to aim in the X and Y axes
  • it will be able to fire non- lethal(ferromagnetic) projectiles up to a certain distance (10 feet is my current goal)
  • It will be able to fire the projectile with varying power
  • it will be able to select targets (this is pushing it slightly)
  • It will be mounted on a moving platform, effectively forming a sort of toy tank (this is pushing it a lot)

what materials and components will be required?

As of now, after having dug around for similar projects, I've come upon an informal list of sub-systems and their components:

  • The movement system:
    • A two axis mount for the servos (that will also hold the coil)
    • two servo motors
    • two servo drivers
    • a physical user interfacre to control the servos (ideally using 4 butons on a board)
    • a means to zero or calibrate the servo positions every time i use the system
  • The firing system
    • A plastic tube
    • Magnet wire
    • An iron bolt almost the size of the tube
    • a high power switch, such as a relay switch, or a thyristor
    • Diodes, to make sure the current doesnt flow the wrong way and wreck something
  • The variable power delivery system
    • Capacitor bank
    • full bridge rectifier
    • charging circuit
    • voltage readout
    • a safe enclosure to avoid death/injury
  • the targetting system (note this is a stretch and I honestly haven't given it too much thought)
    • a camera or simpler sensor (maybe just ultrasound)
    • a board that identifies targets
    • Code defining targetting parameters
    • interface with the servos
